Cassie's Favorite Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time!

Die Hard Series

Great action films with some humor. It also stars my favorite actor Bruce Willis.

Steel Magnolias

The best movie to watch when you need a laugh and a good cry. Yes ladies, we have all been there!

Little Mermaid

The original animation, not the remake. As a beach lover, it is fun to see a storyline of a world under the sea.
